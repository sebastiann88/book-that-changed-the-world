How It Started...

I used to be normal. You know the sort of thing. Outside – happily divorced, unhappily employed, living in a condo townhouse. Inside – tied to the job by money, living a pointless life that would one day Pop! into nothingness.

Then an unexpected book happened. It described how the world really worked, which was entirely different from the way I had been taught. For instance, there is a purpose in living, which is to learn about and improve yourself. And that is not wasted. When you die, it is more of a graduation. You discard your physical body and move to another level of existence. You remain the same person, for better or for worse. One essential part of your new life is to look back at all your actions in your previous one, and decide what you did right and the one or two little details you are not proud of. Incidentally, the mind can communicate between levels. Technology cannot, which is why we cannot detect extraterrestrial life.
